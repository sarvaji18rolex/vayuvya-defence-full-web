TRJ-M1


Product Overview

The TRJ-M1P is an upgraded micro turbojet engine delivering 5kN thrust. It incorporates advanced DEEC with AI optimization to enhance performance and fuel efficiency. Designed for long-range missiles, tactical UAVs, and target drones, the engine ensures consistent thrust, durability, and precise control. With a design life exceeding 25 operational hours, the TRJ-M1P combines reliability, high thrust-to-weight ratio, and cutting-edge engine management.
